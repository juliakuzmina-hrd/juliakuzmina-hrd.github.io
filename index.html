What I Do

  • Build teams around specific goals and create effective ways of working through structure, culture, and processes. Embed cultural principles that reflect the values of founders and senior leadership.
    Case Matrix Structure. During business expansion, teams were organized around products — resulting in 4 separate brands under one roof sharing a back office, which led to fragmented expertise, misaligned metrics, duplicated roles, and inflated payroll. Introduced a matrix structure with a unified metrics system: expertise became shared and mutually reinforcing. Payroll −20% at same revenue
  • Data-driven approach. Build analytics and metrics systems for evidence-based decisions — as a one-time diagnostic or an ongoing system: speed and quality of the hiring process, payroll, process transparency, engagement rate, burnout rate, etc.
    Case Burnout. Managers started flagging burnout across their teams, but there was no way to assess the scale or deviation from baseline — so we built a metric from scratch, set up systematic measurement, tested prevention initiatives through focus groups, and helped those in the critical zone. 25% → 15% in one year70% moved from critical to safe zone
    Case Recruitment Analytics. A single time-to-hire SLA was demotivating recruiters and made hiring planning nearly impossible — we calculated historical conversion data by category and broke the SLA down into stages of the hiring process, setting a target for each one. The result was a planning tool for both recruiters and the wider business, not a monitoring instrument. Avg. Time-to-Offer · 25 days

We rebuilt the hiring process, created new policies and internal regulations, and introduced transparent bonus scenarios that everyone knew in advance: no bonus, standard bonus, or double bonus — depending on results. Payroll became manageable — and as a side effect, the entire team became more mindful of costs and more focused on finding revenue growth opportunities. Forecast accuracy 95%+
